About Us

GIGANT with more than 5 years of experience

"GIGANT" offers services for the design of new and reconstructed buildings and structures, as well as technical inspection and instrumental research of industrial and civil facilities in Tashkent and on the territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan. The purpose of the survey is to comply with the current legislation, according to which, when developing design estimates for buildings and structures to be reconstructed, “it is necessary to carry out a set of measures to ensure their seismic resistance, durability and quality, observing the requirements of republican norms and rules of construction (Art. 15 of the Law of the Republic of Uzbekistan on "Architecture and Construction").
