OUR PHILOSOPHY IS BASED ON A CONCEPTUAL APPROACH INSPIRED BY VISION AND DESIGN

Construction equipment, engineering, planning and innovation

For more than 5 years, "GIGANT" has earned one of the finest reputations across Uzbekistan for construction engineering service that achieve our client’s vision

"GIGANT" offers services for the design of new and reconstructed buildings and structures, as well as technical inspection and instrumental research of industrial and civil facilities in Tashkent and on the territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan. The purpose of the survey is to comply with the current legislation, according to which, when developing design estimates for buildings and structures to be reconstructed, “it is necessary to carry out a set of measures to ensure their seismic resistance, durability and quality, observing the requirements of republican norms and rules of construction (Art. 15 of the Law of the Republic of Uzbekistan on "Architecture and Construction").

The purpose of the survey of buildings and structures is - a reliable assessment of the technical condition and performance; preliminary (selective) and detailed (instrumental) studies of building structures: determining the causes and nature of damage received by a building or structure during the period of operation; computational analysis of structures for limiting states on the influence of static and dynamic (seismic) influences: performance of work on the study and determination of soils of foundations and foundations (development by the drilling method); issuance of decisions and recommendations in connection with an additional superstructure of storeys or a change in the layout. Laboratory determination and assessment of the actual concrete strength of the bearing structures of the building of prefabricated and monolithic reinforced concrete columns and girders, instrumental research by a non-destructive method using the device.
