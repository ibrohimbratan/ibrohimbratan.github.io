Planning:

Designing any object requires specific knowledge and special skills. This is a conceptually complex set of activities based on innovative technical solutions, requiring the use of the latest technologies and taking into account the specifics of the customer’s business. A competently and professionally drawn up project guarantees the coherence of the object’s work in the future. Cooperation with LLC PMP “StroyTsekhTekhnologiya” is the only sure way to avoid possible losses and establish efficient production. meat processing shops for partial or complete processing of cattle meat; dairy shops, farms and factories for the processing of various types of milk; mini-bakeries for the production of various bakery and confectionery products. Only a well-designed production project in compliance with all technical standards can ensure successful activities in the farming business, reaching a new level today. The design of such an assignment of objects, which is based on the principle of economic feasibility, requires high professionalism and a special approach – our engineers, designers and architects cope with the solution of the tasks assigned to them at the highest level. StroyTsekhTechnologii specialists carry out all work from A to Z without the involvement of contractors – starting from a preliminary design and creating design and working documentation, ending with approval, passing an examination and obtaining a construction permit. It is at the design stage that a decision is made about the range of future products and their volumes, and schemes of future technological processes are drawn. Our experienced designers will provide all the subtleties of work, both small and large-scale production, ensuring optimal and efficient use of resources and the highest possible profitability. “StroyTsekhTekhnologiya” will consider all the options for the breakdown of power lines, water supply, sewerage and other utilities in order to find the best option for you. We will pay special attention to the location of auxiliary, storage, administrative infrastructure facilities and settlements. StroyTsekhTechnologii specialists will develop a functional economic zone and a platform for the utilization of raw materials. StroyTsekhTekhnologiya will develop a technological scheme for the operation of the facility in accordance with GOSTs, OSTs, technical regulations. We will draw up a specification of equipment, taking into account all stages of production. StroyTsekhTekhnologiya carries out design and construction from the initial stage – the formation of an idea – until the moment when the object will be officially handed over to the customer. The high professionalism of our engineers and architects, as well as their use of innovative technologies in the field of construction and design, allows us to guarantee the minimum terms for the construction of facilities in compliance with all applicable GOST and SanPiN standards while maintaining the high quality of work. The company “LLC PMP StroyTsekhTekhnologiya” is engaged in the creation of: standard projects that are universal and used for the construction of objects of one purpose; individual projects – multifunctional, taking into account all the needs of the customer.
