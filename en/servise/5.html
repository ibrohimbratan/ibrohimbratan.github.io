Interior and Landscape design

Interior design is created to decorate a room, to make it beautiful and comfortable. Its development is one of the design stages before renovation, reconstruction or finishing of a new facility. At the same time, they solve problems that are related to the purpose, layout of premises, their engineering equipment, zoning, visual solutions, furniture arrangement, compliance with the features of the chosen style. Design requirements will be different for apartments and residential buildings, offices, restaurants or shops: in some cases, comfort is important, and in others – convenience, brightness, memorability Our studio has been designing and equipping residential and public premises for more than 5 years. Over the years, we have established ourselves as responsible and competent masters of our craft, because our team consists of designers and architects with rich experience, creative, but at the same time scrupulous attitude to business. Whether it is an apartment, an office or a country house – you can always contact us and count on a full range of services: measurements, formation of a design project, sketches, modeling, selection of materials and furniture, calculation of costs, complete decoration of the premises, etc. In addition, you can always use our additional services, such as architectural supervision, decoration, etc.
